# LDPE and HDPE Recyclers in Germany: Capacities, Prices, Technologies

## Leading Recycling Companies

### ALBA Group (Berlin)
Capacity: 120,000 tonnes/year (LDPE films and rigid HDPE)

### REMONDIS Recycling (Lünen)
HDPE fraction: 85,000 tonnes/year, food-grade regranulate output

### PreZero (Neustadt)
Specialization: post-consumer blends, RecyClass certification

### mtm plastics (Niedergebra)
Europe's largest mixed film recycling plant — 75,000 tonnes LDPE/year

## Secondary Polyethylene Prices in Germany (2024, ex-works, EUR/t)
| Type | Price |
|------|-------|
| Clear LDPE flakes | 720–850 |
| Black LDPE regranulate | 620–740 |
| Natural HDPE (washed) | 900–1000 |
| HDPE food-grade regranulate | 980–1150 |
| HDPE technical | 700–820 |

## Regulatory Requirements for Recyclers
- EuCertPlast certification
- Traceability (ISO 22030)
- Recyclate declaration in Grüner Punkt system

## Technologies
- Hot washing + flotation separation (HDPE)
- Agglomeration and granulation with two-stage filtration (LDPE)
- Deodorization (for food-grade regranulates)

## Where Recyclate from German Processors Is Used
End products: Automotive mats (HDPE), Construction film (LDPE), Furniture panels.

## Conclusion
Germany remains the technology hub for PE recycling in the EU. Shortage of high-quality feedstock pushes prices up by 5–7% annually.
