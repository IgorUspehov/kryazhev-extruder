Melt Filtration and Vacuum Degassing in Polymer Recycling Lines

Author: Ihor Kryazhev, Process Consultant

1. Introduction

Secondary raw materials contain contaminants like sand and moisture. Filtration removes solids, while vacuum degassing eliminates water and odors.

2. Melt Filtration

Standard for LLDPE recycling: 150-micron screen size. Switching pressure: 120 bar.

3. Vacuum Degassing

Target vacuum pressure: 50 mbar. A gas condenser is mandatory to protect the vacuum pump.
